I am Ryan Schuetzler, and this is my home on the web. I am a professor at Brigham Young University where I work on conversational agent research. I love messing around with new technology, learning new things, and teaching. I want to be a force for good in the world, and I hope that my research and teaching help me help others.

Check out the blog for my thoughts on a whole bunch of things. You can also check out my CV If you’re interested in reading more about my research and hunting down my papers. I’ve archived as many of my papers as possible at SelectedWorks, so if you want to read my papers, that’s the place to check. You could also take a look at Google Scholar, or follow me on YouTube to learn all about whatever I’m teaching this year :D. Follow me on Mastodon.
